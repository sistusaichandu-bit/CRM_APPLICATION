ℹ️ Patient Registration Guide

When registering a new patient:

  • Ensure the patient's full name matches their identification
  • Select the appropriate medical problem category for initial diagnosis
  • Assign a qualified doctor based on specialization
  • Set the initial status (Active = routine care, Follow-up = needs revisit, Referred = specialist needed)
  • The system will track all visits and referrals for this patient
